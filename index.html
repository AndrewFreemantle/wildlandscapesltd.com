Wild Landscapes are an established soft landscape contractor based in York

We have over 16 years experience in contract work for the commercial sector

We pride ourselves on producing and maintaining high quality projects to a budget and within agreed timescales for construction contractors, landscape architects and housing associations. From our base in York, we service projects throughout the Yorkshire region.

Wild Landscapes believe that good health and safety practices are paramount onsite. We are proud to be CHAS accredited; CHAS is a market leader in health and safety prequalification. All are highly trained staff are fully CSCS certified with plant operatives holding CPCS cards and NPTC cards.
